Who we are

The Farage Money Monitor is an independent project that tracks the declared financial interests of Nigel Farage MP using Parliament's public register. The site and its sign-up list are operated by Athrach Ltd (registered in England and Wales, company number 16906800), which is the data controller for any personal information you give us. To exercise any of your rights described below, contact: brian.whelan.journalism@gmail.com.

What we collect, and why

If you fill in the sign-up box, we collect your first name, email address and postcode. We use them for one purpose only: to contact you about opposing Reform UK where you live, and to connect you with others doing the same.

Because signing up indicates a political opinion, the law treats these details as special category data. We rely on your explicit consent, which you give by submitting the form — and you can withdraw it at any time by emailing us or replying "unsubscribe" to anything we send.

What we don't do

  • We never sell, rent, swap or share your details with anyone.
  • We don't use them for anything beyond the purpose above.
  • We don't add you to any other list.

Where your details live

Form submissions are stored securely by Netlify, the service that hosts this website, and copies arrive in our private inbox. We keep them only while the project is active or until you ask us to delete them, whichever comes first.

Cookies and visiting this site

This website sets no cookies and runs no analytics or advertising trackers. Like almost every website, our hosting provider records standard technical logs (such as IP addresses) to keep the site running securely, and the page loads its typefaces from Google Fonts, which involves your browser requesting files from Google's servers.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time to show you what we hold about you, correct it, or delete it entirely — just email the address above and we'll do it promptly. If you're unhappy with how we've handled your data, you can complain to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.
