Not on the register

A reported £5 million gift from crypto billionaire Christopher Harborne does not appear in Farage's register of interests — and is therefore not included in any total on this page. First reported by The Guardian in April 2026, the payment is the subject of an ongoing investigation by the Parliamentary Commissioner for Standards into whether it should have been declared. Farage says he was under no obligation to register it because it was given before he became an MP. The register does record Harborne separately funding a £25,000 flight to the Maldives in February 2026 (see visits below).

Shareholdings — including Farage's stakes in Stack BTC PLC, Farage Media Ltd and Thorn In The Side Ltd — carry no published value in the register, so they are not counted here either.
